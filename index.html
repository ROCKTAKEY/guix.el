Emacs-Guix

About

Emacs-Guix (aka guix.el) is an Emacs interface for GNU Guix package manager.

Emacs-Guix allows you to manage your Guix profile(s): to install, upgrade and remove packages, to switch and remove profile generations, to display all available info about packages and to do many other things.

Source code: https://github.com/alezost/guix.el.
